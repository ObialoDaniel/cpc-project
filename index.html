CALIFORNIA PREPARATORY COLLEGE

California Preparatory College (CPC) is a private, faith-based, two-year college that offers students a high degree of individual mentoring through life counselors, small classroom sizes, and a dedicated staff and faculty that have a real passion for Christian higher education.
