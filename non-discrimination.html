RELIGIOUS/ NON-DISCRIMINATION

CPC is a private, nonprofit Christian college located in Colton, California. CPC is not corporately affiliated with an organized religion or faith, but its culture and educational philosophy is founded upon the tenants of the Seventh-day Adventist belief system. While CPC is a Christian college, it welcomes students, faculty and staff from all backgrounds, denominations and beliefs.

California Preparatory College does not discriminate against students or employees on the basis of race, color, national or ethnic origin, sex, disability, age, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity or any other legally protected status.
